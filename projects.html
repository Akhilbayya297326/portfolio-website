Krishi Chakra 2.0

The Neural Matrix of Agriculture

"While the world discusses AI for Silicon Valley, we looked at the backbone of our nation. Most agri-tech apps today are just digital noticeboards—they don't think, predict, or protect. Krishi Chakra transforms the traditional farmer into a data-driven enterprise manager."

Smart AgriCore & Commercial Gateway

  • Subscription Gate: A scalable revenue model. Users unlock the 'Pro Matrix' via dynamic backend authentication.
  • Geospatial Telemetry: Automatically locks onto the farmer's satellite location, cross-referencing local groundwater tables.
  • The Business Matrix: Calculates Expected Revenue vs. Net Profit per acre based on live Mandi trends.

Active Care & Resource Sharing

  • Pest Radar: Uses a Haversine Distance Algorithm to map nearby threats, triggering Voice Alerts within a 10km radius.
  • Hydro Smart: A Life-Cycle Irrigation Matrix that saves up to 50% groundwater.
  • Sahayog (Agri-Uber): A secure booking engine for machinery sharing with Unified UPI QR codes.

MediFlow AI

Intelligent Hospital Management System

"Traditional systems are highly fragmented. Patients struggle to find specialists, beds are tracked on static clipboards, and legacy software lacks backend security. MediFlow bridges the gap between public accessibility and enterprise-grade administrative control."

Public Gateway & Live Tracking

  • Symptom-Based AI Routing: Patients bypass medical jargon. Typing "severe chest pain" dynamically filters the DB to match a Cardiologist.
  • Live Resource Tracking (CRUD): Dynamic DB tracking 100+ hospital beds. Doctors update patient status which instantly refreshes public metrics.

Enterprise Security (RBAC)

  • Role-Based Access: System intercepts logins, routing ADMINS to the Command Center and DOCTORS to the Clinical Dashboard.
  • Fail-Safe Protection: The SecurityFilterChain instantly intercepts unauthorized endpoint access and redirects to the secure login gateway.

ApexResume

Live Demo

Intelligent ATS Scoring & Optimization Engine

"Job seekers often submit resumes that fail ATS screening due to weak structure and missing skill alignment. ApexResume was built to solve this by providing actionable, AI-driven feedback loops to bridge the gap between candidates and recruiters."

The Approach

  • AI Analysis Pipeline: Integrated the Gemini API and NLP to deeply analyze resume structures, providing an instant, highly accurate ATS score.
  • Personalized Roadmaps: Generates job-role specific suggestions and visualizes a complete skill roadmap for the user.
  • Company-Specific Generation: Allows users to tailor and generate resumes mapped directly to specific company requirements.
  • Gamification: Implemented a real-time leaderboard to foster continuous improvement among job seekers.

Achieved massive engagement spikes by replacing generic advice with highly personalized, actionable feedback loops and role-focused optimization guidance.
